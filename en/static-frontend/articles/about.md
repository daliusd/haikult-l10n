Hi! I'm Dalius, a software developer and the person behind Haiku.lt.

I originally built Haiku.lt for my wife when I couldn't find an invoicing tool that was simple enough for her needs. Most tools were either too complicated, too expensive, or tried to do too much. I wanted something that just worked - fast, clean, and without the bloat.

What started as a personal project has grown into a tool that I hope helps other individual professionals manage their invoicing with minimal friction. I'm committed to keeping Haiku.lt simple and high-quality, focusing on what matters most: helping you get your invoices done quickly and correctly.

Right now, Haiku.lt is a side project, but depending on how it evolves and what users need, that might change in the future.

If you have questions, feedback, or just want to say hi, feel free to reach out at [dalius.dobravolskas@gmail.com](mailto:dalius.dobravolskas@gmail.com).
