Haiku.lt is built following this philosophy:

- Tools for individual professionals should be:

  - **Simple** - Haiku.lt focuses on essential functionality without unnecessary bloat.

  - **Fast** - Haiku.lt is designed to minimize the time you spend on invoicing tasks.

  - **Flexible** - Haiku.lt avoids artificial limitations that would prevent you from
    accomplishing your tasks.

  - **Error-preventing** - Flexibility can create opportunities for mistakes, so Haiku.lt
    helps you avoid common errors where possible.

  - **Accessible everywhere** - Haiku.lt works on both desktop computers and
    mobile devices.

- The free plan will never have less functionality than it currently has, unless
  circumstances beyond our control increase the cost of maintaining the free tier.
  In any case, we will inform you of any changes.

- The paid plan provides these benefits:

  - More functionality allowing you to work faster and accomplish more

  - Priority support when you have questions or issues

  - Consideration of your feature requests
