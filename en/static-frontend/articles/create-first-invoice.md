Creating your first invoice in Haikult takes less than a minute. Open the app, click **New Invoice**, and follow these five steps.

## Step 1: Main Information

![Main information](/screenshots/create-first-invoice/en/step-1-main-info.png)

The **Series Name** groups your invoices under a common prefix (e.g. `INV`). The series number increments automatically with each new invoice, so you never have to worry about numbering. The date defaults to today — adjust it if the invoice covers a different period. Leave the remaining fields as they are for now.

## Step 2: Seller

![Seller information](/screenshots/create-first-invoice/en/step-2-seller.png)

Enter your business or personal details: name, registration number, and VAT number if applicable. This information is saved to your account and pre-filled automatically on every future invoice.

## Step 3: Buyer

![Buyer information](/screenshots/create-first-invoice/en/step-3-buyer.png)

Enter the buyer's name and email address. The email is used when sending the invoice directly from the app. Previously used buyers appear as suggestions — select one to fill the fields instantly.

## Step 4: Services or Products

![Services or products](/screenshots/create-first-invoice/en/step-4-line-items.png)

Add at least one line item: enter a description, quantity, and unit price. The row total is calculated automatically. Click the **+** button to add more rows for additional services or products.

## Step 5: Invoice Management

![Invoice management](/screenshots/create-first-invoice/en/step-5-controls.png)

Click the button to create the invoice. It will appear in your invoices list and a PDF will be generated automatically, ready to download or send to the buyer.
